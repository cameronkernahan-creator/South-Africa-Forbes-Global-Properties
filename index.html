Investors receive 20% of annual distributable profits once the company is profitable and a four-month operating reserve has been established. The business experiences startup losses in Years 1 to 3 while revenue scales, with Year 4 profits used to rebuild capital and establish reserves. Dividend distributions begin in Year 5. After the six-year term, investor capital of R3,000,000 is returned in full. Total projected cash received: approximately R8,574,000 representing a 2.85x return multiple and an estimated IRR of 18 to 19% per annum.
