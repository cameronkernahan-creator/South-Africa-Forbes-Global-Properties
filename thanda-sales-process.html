The Royal Thanda Club
Sales Process

01 — The Opportunity

The Royal Thanda Club comprises 44 private residence memberships within a 14,000-hectare Big Five reserve in KwaZulu-Natal, South Africa. 7 memberships have sold off-market. 16 remain across Phase 1 and Phase 2. No formal sales process has been conducted to date. All sales have resulted from personal relationships and direct introductions. Remaining inventory value: ZAR 3.8bn to ZAR 5bn. This document outlines the first structured sales process for these memberships.

02 — The Sales Process

Six steps. Personal contact at every stage.
No mass outreach. No advertising. No cold leads.

1

We identify 9 Forbes Global Properties network members across 6 priority regions: US, Middle East, Scandinavia, Germany/Switzerland, UK, and one additional market to be confirmed with Forbes Global Properties CEO Marcus. These are handpicked, top-tier advisors with direct access to UHNW clients.

2

We bring these members to Thanda in groups of 3, across 3 months (May, June, July). Each group spends 3 days on the reserve. They experience the product firsthand. They leave as advocates.

3

Each activated member returns to their market and personally engages their network. Based on industry benchmarks, each member is expected to surface 5 qualified prospects from their direct client relationships.

4

Keegan Steyn (Principal Broker, South Africa | Forbes Global Properties) personally qualifies every international prospect. He conducts due diligence, facilitates video introductions, and manages the relationship until the prospect is ready to visit.

5

Qualified prospects sign a reservation agreement and pay a site visit deposit before travelling. Pierre Delvaux and the Thanda team host them for a curated 3-day experience on the reserve.

6

Sale agreement and building contract, facilitated by Thanda's conveyancers and attorneys.

03 — Activity Targets

These are conservative numbers. Referral-sourced leads in ultra-luxury real estate convert at 15 to 20%, compared to 0.5 to 2% for cold digital leads. The network model is 10x more efficient by design.

05 — What Thanda Provides

Hosted accommodation for network member familiarisation visits: 3 days per group, 3 groups. Hosted accommodation for qualified prospect site visits, funded by the prospect's deposit. Deposit is deducted from the purchase price on sale, retained if the prospect does not proceed. Existing presentation material, photography, and property documentation for network members to use in client conversations. Pierre's availability for prospect calls and site visits as required.

06 — Proof of Concept

The first sale through this process validates the model. One deal proves the process works. From there, we scale: more members activated, more markets opened, more site visits, more sales.

Dan's own experience confirms this. Dr Morsel, Thanda's first outside buyer, was a stranger on an ultra-high-net-worth database. He received a teaser. He responded. He was qualified. He visited. He bought. That is exactly the process we are formalising and scaling through the Forbes network.
